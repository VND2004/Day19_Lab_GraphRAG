OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, John Schulman, Wojciech Zaremba, and Elon Musk as a non-profit research organization.
Microsoft invested in OpenAI and integrated OpenAI models into Azure Cloud services.
Google is owned by Alphabet Inc. and developed Gemini as a multimodal AI assistant.
NVIDIA develops GPUs that are widely used for training and inference of large language models.
Meta developed Llama and released several open-weight language models to the research community.
Sam Altman is the CEO of OpenAI and previously worked at Y Combinator as president.
Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, and co-founded OpenAI with Sam Altman.
Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft and has led the company's AI transformation strategy.
Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google and oversees the development of Gemini.
Jensen Huang is the CEO and founder of NVIDIA, driving GPU technology for AI.
ChatGPT is a conversational AI product developed by OpenAI and launched in November 2022.
GitHub Copilot uses OpenAI's Codex model and is owned by Microsoft.
Azure is Microsoft's cloud platform that provides AI services powered by OpenAI models.
CUDA is NVIDIA's parallel computing platform used for deep learning and scientific computing.
Y Combinator invested in OpenAI during its early stages and mentored the company.
OpenAI released GPT-4, a large language model that powers ChatGPT Pro and various enterprise applications.
Tesla developed Autopilot, an autonomous driving system powered by neural networks.
Google Cloud provides AI services including Vertex AI for machine learning workflows.
Meta's LLaMA models are used by researchers and companies for fine-tuning custom AI applications.
Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides SageMaker for machine learning and competes with Azure and Google Cloud.
OpenAI partnered with Microsoft to integrate GPT models into Office 365 and Teams.
NVIDIA A100 GPUs are the standard for training large language models in data centers.
DeepMind, owned by Alphabet, conducts AI research and developed AlphaGo and AlphaFold.
Anthropic was founded by former members of OpenAI and develops Claude, an AI assistant.
Google Search integrates AI features powered by Gemini for better query understanding.
